Grand Theft Auto V


Grand Theft Auto V is a action/adventure game and the fifteenth release in the series if you include the expansion packs for Grand Theft Auto 1 and Grand Theft Auto IV and non-PC releases but the eleventh GTA game or expansion released for the PC. The game returns players to San Andreas, the fictional state based on the states of California and Nevada, and same setting for â€‹Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. It was initially released for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 in September 2013 and then for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One consoles a year later. It was finally released for the PC in March 2015 with enhancements that take full advantage of processing and graphical power of PCs. These enhancements in the PC version of the game include greater graphical detail, support for higher screen resolutions, denser traffic, updated AI, enhanced weather effects and more.

Grand Theft Auto V contains the same open world design that is found in every other Grand Theft Auto release. GTA 5 differs a little in the fact that it can be played from the third person view or first person view and the fact that players will switch between three different main characters. The game world of GTA 5 includes the city of Los Santos and the surrounding areas and is hte largest gaming world to date in the series. As with previous titles, players will unlock different parts of the game world as the progress through the story based missions but will have the ability to take on side missions and jobs which can be completed at their leisure. The story revoles around the three protagonists and criminals, Michael De Santa, Trevor Philips and Franklin Clinton, who engage in criminal activity under threat from rival government agencies. GTA 5 also includes an RPG style aspect in the fact that each character has a set of skills and abilities that can be improved as they gain experience as they player through the game.

The online multiplayer component for GTA 5, known as Grand Theft Auto Online, is somewhat of a stand alone offering in addition to the single player story campaign. It is a persistent game world where players will create a unique character and offers various game play including racing, death match and objective based multiplayer missions. It also contains leveling which allows players to be more guns, vehicles and missions. It also includes its unlockable achievements for both the single and multiplayer games
